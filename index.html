A little bit about me.

G'day from Sydney, Australia! I'm originally from the UK but relocated to sunny Sydney a few years ago and have never looked back. My interest in working in tech came from running my own business in the UK before my big move, when I had to set up and manage my small business website.

Although I found it super challenging, I discovered a real passion for building websites that are clean, modern and user-friendly. I set this site up to capture some of my work and to showcase what I am able to offer to potential clients.
